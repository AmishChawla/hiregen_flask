Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our recruitment solutions

What is HireGen?

HireGen is an AI-powered recruitment platform that helps businesses streamline their hiring process. We offer comprehensive solutions including applicant tracking, resume parsing, and AI-driven candidate matching.

How does AI improve the recruitment process?

Our AI technology automates candidate screening, provides intelligent matching between job requirements and candidate profiles, and offers data-driven insights to make better hiring decisions.

What features are included in the platform?

Our platform includes features such as applicant tracking, resume parsing, AI candidate matching, recruitment CRM, interview scheduling, and comprehensive analytics.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial period to test our platform's features. You can sign up for a trial through our website to experience the full capabilities of our recruitment solution.

How secure is my data?

We prioritize data security and comply with industry standards. All data is encrypted, and we follow strict privacy protocols to protect your information.
