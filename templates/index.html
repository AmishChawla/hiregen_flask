Features that define Hiregen's excellence.

ATS

Designed to streamline and manage the recruitment process, speed up hiring by automating repetitive tasks and provides a visual dashboard to track progress in real-time.

  • Collects job applications and stores candidate data in a centralized database
  • Tracks candidate progress e.g., applied, interviewing, offered
  • Analyzes resumes to rank candidates based on skills and job fit
  • Eliminates manual resume sifting, replacing email chaos or paper stacks.

Automated Resume Screening

AI scans and evaluates resumes by extracting key information like skills, experience, and qualifications, then ranks candidates based on how well they match the job requirements

  • AI scans resumes to extract key details like skills, experience, and qualifications
  • Evaluates and ranks candidates based on job requirement matches
  • Processes large application volumes quickly and efficiently
  • Saves time, allowing recruiters to focus on top candidates without manual sorting

Candidate Matching

Uses algorithms to pair candidates with job openings by analyzing skills, experience, and even cultural fit, often going beyond basic keyword matching to assess potential.

  • Analyzes skills, experience, and cultural fit using advanced algorithms
  • Goes beyond keywords to assess candidate potential
  • Pairs candidates with suitable job openings automatically.
  • Improves hire quality by reducing mismatches and finding the best fits

Job Description Optimization

AI generates or refines job postings by suggesting language that's clear, inclusive, and appealing, sometimes analyzing past successful postings for inspiration.

  • Generates or refines job postings with clear, inclusive language.
  • Suggests phrasing based on successful past postings.
  • Reduces biased or unclear wording proactively
  • Attracts diverse talent and makes postings more effective.

Personalized Outreach

Crafts tailored emails or messages to candidates based on their profiles, skills, or interests

  • Crafts tailored emails or messages using candidate profile data.
  • Incorporates skills, interests, or experience for relevance.
  • Uses natural language to feel human and engaging
  • Increases response rates and candidate interest

Analytics

Provides real-time insights into recruitment metrics like time-to-hire, source effectiveness, or candidate pipeline health through intuitive visualizations.

  • Tracks metrics like time-to-hire or source effectiveness.
  • Presents data in real-time via intuitive visualizations.
  • Highlights pipeline health and recruitment trends.
  • Provides actionable insights to refine strategies and report progress.

Interview Scheduling

Online Interview has revolutionized the hiring process, enabling businesses to streamline candidate assessments, improve decision-making, and reduce hiring time.

  • Syncs with recruiter and candidate calendars to find available slots
  • Sends automated invites and reminders via email
  • Adjusts schedules if conflicts arise, notifying all parties.
