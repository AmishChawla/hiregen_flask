Key Features of HireGen’s Resume Parsing Software

1. AI-Powered Resume Extraction

  • Advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) extracts key candidate details.
  • Identifies and categorizes skills, experience, education, and contact details.
  • Multi-language support for global recruitment needs.

2. Accurate Candidate Data Structuring

  • Converts unstructured resume data into a standardized format.
  • Extracts job titles, locations, and industries for precise filtering.
  • Minimizes errors by eliminating manual data entry.

3. Multi-Format Resume Processing

  • Supports PDF, DOC, DOCX, RTF, TXT, and image-based resumes.
  • OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology for scanned resumes.

  • AI-driven resume-to-job matching based on job descriptions.
  • Keyword-based search for fast talent identification.

5. Seamless ATS & CRM Integration

  • Directly integrates with HireGen’s Applicant Tracking System (ATS).
  • API-based connectivity with HRMS, payroll, and CRM platforms.
